Why bilingual stories?

One story. Three languages. Endless ways to connect.

Every book is crafted to be read aloud, shared, and lingered over — whether your family speaks English at the dinner table, Spanish at bedtime, or a little of both. Several titles are also available in Italian, so the same beloved story can travel from one home to another, in whatever language feels like home.
