About the Author

Dr. Ornella Lira Spinali De Figueredo

English Español Italiano

Ornella is an American author with deep roots stretching across Italy and Venezuela — a blend of cultures that shapes every story she tells.

By day, she works as a physician specializing in Occupational Health. By night (and nap time, and the in-between moments), she's a mom putting her own family's bedtime routines, road trips, and kitchen-table traditions onto the page.

"The home is the foundation for developing each individual's potential."

Ornella believes that wholeheartedly — and puts it to the test every day through her own experience of motherhood. Her books are written and published in English, Spanish, and Italian, inviting parents and children to read side by side, explore the world's flavors and cultures, and find comfort, curiosity, and connection in everyday moments.

Whether it's a puppy finding his way home, a family gathered for Christmas, or two young explorers discovering a planet that can be felt rather than just seen, every story carries the same quiet hope: healthy minds, happy homes, and a love of reading that lasts a lifetime.
