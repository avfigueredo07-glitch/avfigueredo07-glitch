Strawberry Rivers on the Dark Planet

Chapter Book · Ages 7–11

Two young explorers crash-land on a mysterious planet — and discover that some worlds aren't just inhabited, they're felt.

Read the full story

In a distant corner of the galaxy, two very different explorers accept a mission that could change history forever: to travel to the mysterious "Dark Planet," a place known only through rumors of strange fruits and possible signs of life. Ranger 005, a solitary scientist with a love for maps and the stars, and young Ranger 003, an enthusiastic biologist who dreams of helping his family, find themselves stranded on an icy, silent, and seemingly dead planet. Cut off from communication, low on supplies, and surrounded by peril, they must learn to survive as they traverse dark mountains and impossible landscapes. But when a strange star begins to illuminate the sky and the planet awakens in magical, inexplicable ways, they discover that this world harbors a secret far greater than they ever imagined — one they must uncover together, bound by shared emotions and friendship.
